Four changes made (targeting the FP-heavy reduction loops):

Change	Location	What	Why
1	UNPACK_HDR_WV_K2 kr loop	Removed UNROLL	Was 16 simultaneous fp16_to_fp32 (2 kr × 8 b); now 8. MAC_ALL_K2 still keeps both kr and b UNROLL'd (safe — UNPACK always completes before MAC starts).
2	REDUCE_WV_K2 b loop	UNROLL → factor=2	Reduces parallel FP chains from 8 to 2. kr is already sequential; 2 kr × 4 b-passes × 2 simultaneous = same arithmetic, ~48 extra cycles per call (negligible vs 256 MAC).
3	REDUCE_DOWN_Q4K	factor=8 → factor=2	32 blocks in 16 sequential passes of 2 instead of 4 passes of 8. Adds ~80 cycles of FP reduction per output row vs 1024 MAC cycles.
4	REDUCE_Q6K	factor=8 → factor=2	Same rationale as above for Q6K path.
